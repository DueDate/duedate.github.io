Our Motive

Women often go through their first pregnancy not knowing what to expect. And many of these women are young teens who are the most at risk for not getting timely prenatal care. Our goal is to provide useful meal plans that are simple and easy to follow, for those who need guidance on how their diet should change during pregnancy. We also want to provide answers to general questions they may have.
