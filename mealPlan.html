Meal Plan

Good nutrition remains vital during pregnancy for both the mother and the baby’s growth and development. In fact, the mother should be consuming around 300 more healthy calories a day than pre-pregnancy. This can lead to many different level of weight gain based on the woman’s prior weight before pregnancy. For an average weight woman prior to pregnancy, the weight gain should be 25-35 pounds while overweight woman would gain 15-25 pounds and underweight woman would gain 28-40 pounds.
